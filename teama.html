AR health

 
 
 

Measuring blood pressure, changing wound dressings and administering medication are just some of the daily functions of a nurse working in internments. Currently, these professionals must look for the best clinical proceedings for each patient and register every intervention. In order to make this process easier, Nebula is developing a mobile app, for Android and iOS, which will allow the nurse to identify a set of patterns and replace them for an augmented reality. Using AR-codes, the nurse, going into a room, quickly sees pending tasks for each patient, registering whatever necessary. Nebula presents a revolutionary project which intends of improving nurse's work-flow and, consequently, improving the services given to the patients.
