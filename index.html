Projects

 
 
 

AR for Medical Care

With the use of Augmented Reality, we aim to bring a suitable replacement for the paper forms in the healthcare department, by having doctors and nurses making use of their smartphones to easily and intuitively access data related to their patients.

Alexa

A Critical Manufacturing project that intends on revolutionizing human-machine interaction. The objective is optimizing the maintenance process e production inside a company.

Speech Recognition

The aim of this project is to develop a mood classification API for Talkdesk, with the goal of improving the rating of calls to call centers. This will allow an optimized communication between companies and clients.

Fammeal

Child obesity is one of the main concerns regarding public health in the 21st century. It’s essential to create healthy behaviors in order to lead them into a better future. Fammeal is the solution to help children to learn how to eat in a healthy and fun way, through serious games.
