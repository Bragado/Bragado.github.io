About Us

 
 
 
 

In Nebula, the primacy for the best service quality is one of our main goals. This Quality Manual is what defines the way our company chose to achieve this goal, in a way that might be analysed by customers, stakeholders and workers so that the Quality Management System that operates is well understood by them all. As a guideline to formulate it, ISO 9001:2015 seemed to us as the best possible way to define the most appropriate politics, practices and rules to follow from the moment of its formulation on. This Manual addresses how the organization is structured, all relevant topics on the quality management system, processes, procedures and the authorities addressed to each process and procedure. The following procedures are based on a philosophy of Plan, Do, Check and Learn such as in customer satisfaction and continual improvement as the most important goals to be achieved. The principles that were followed to create this manual and all quality management system were having as main focus the customer, leadership, commitment to people, processes approach and continuous improvement, seen by us as fundamental points to achieve success in maintaining the best quality when delivering our service.
